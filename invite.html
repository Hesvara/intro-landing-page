How it works

From sign-up to your first session — here's what to expect.

1

Join the beta

Fill out the short form above. You'll get immediate access — no waiting, no approval step.

2

Create your secure account

Because you'll be uploading real healthcare documents, we take security seriously. You'll set up your account with email verification and multi-factor authentication — an extra step, but an important one to keep your information safe.

3

Upload a few documents

Start with 3–5 documents you already have — insurance letters, medical records, IEPs, denial notices. This is what makes the beta meaningful and lets you see Hesvara working with your real situation.

4

Explore and share your thoughts

Try the tools. See what's useful, notice what's missing, flag what's confusing. Use the Beta Feedback page inside the app to share your thoughts throughout the beta — your honest feedback is the whole point.

A note on the extra security steps. We know creating accounts can feel like a hassle — but caregiving documents contain some of the most sensitive information there is. The authentication steps exist to make sure no one but you can access your data. It takes a few extra minutes upfront so you never have to worry about it after.
