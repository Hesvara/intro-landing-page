About Hesvara

An AI-powered advocacy and administration hub helping family caregivers organize documents, track deadlines, and create professional advocacy materials.

What We Do

Hesvara is a caregiver advocacy platform designed to address the overwhelming administrative burden faced by America's 63 million family caregivers. We combine intelligent document management, proactive deadline tracking, and AI-powered advocacy drafting into a single, easy-to-use application.

Our platform serves caregivers navigating complex systems like IEP (Individualized Education Program) processes, Medicaid applications, insurance appeals, and benefits coordination. Instead of juggling spreadsheets, email folders, and handwritten notes, caregivers can use Hesvara to keep everything organized and ready when it matters most.

Our mission: Give caregivers back the time and energy they need to focus on what matters most-caring for their loved ones.

The Problem We're Solving

Family caregivers in the United States provide an estimated $600 billion in unpaid care annually. Beyond the physical and emotional demands, caregivers face an enormous administrative burden: tracking deadlines, organizing medical records, coordinating with multiple agencies, and advocating for their loved one's rights.

Our Platform

Hesvara consists of five interconnected modules, each addressing a specific aspect of caregiver administration. Together, they create a comprehensive system that reduces overwhelm and enables confident advocacy.

🗼

Watchtower

Proactive deadline monitoring with intelligent alerts for IEP meetings, insurance renewals, Medicaid recertifications, and more.

📋

CaseFile

Intelligent document management with automatic categorization, secure storage, and instant search across all records.

📝

Care Log

Daily behavior and incident tracking that builds an evidence trail over time, surfacing patterns that inform advocacy.

⚖️

CaseMaker

AI-powered drafting of professional advocacy letters, insurance appeals, IEP requests, and benefits applications.

🧠

AI Insights

Surface patterns, predict risks, and get intelligent suggestions that connect evidence across all your data.
