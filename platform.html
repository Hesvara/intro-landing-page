Five Modules, One Mission

Each module handles a specific piece of the caregiving puzzle. Together, they give you back your time.

Hesvara is built around five tightly integrated modules. You can move between them without re-entering data, and the work you do in one strengthens every other — a note in Care Log feeds patterns in AI Insights, a document in CaseFile becomes evidence for an appeal in CaseMaker, a deadline you forwarded in surfaces as an alert in Watchtower.

The result is a single, calm administrative hub instead of a dozen open tabs, sticky notes, and email threads.

🗼

Watchtower

Never miss a deadline. Intelligent monitoring alerts you to upcoming IEP meetings, insurance renewals, and coverage risks before they become emergencies.

📋

CaseFile

Every record organized and accessible. Medical reports, IEPs, insurance documents — all searchable and ready when you need them.

📝

Care Log

Capture what really happens day-to-day. Build an evidence trail of behaviors, incidents, and patterns that strengthens your advocacy.

⚖️

CaseMaker

Transform your evidence into powerful letters. AI-generated appeals, IEP requests, and benefit applications — professional advocacy at your fingertips.

🧠

AI Insights

Surface patterns, predict risks, and get intelligent suggestions that connect evidence across all your data.
