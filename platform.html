CaseFile

Every record, sorted by the categories you actually use.

Forward documents by email, drag them into the browser, or snap a photo from your phone. CaseFile sorts them into the categories that show up in real caregiver paperwork, and processes each file for searchable text and key dates.

  • Categories: Medical Records, Insurance/EOBs, IEP/504 Plans, Legal Documents, School Records, Therapy Notes, Other
  • Drop PDFs, images, or Word docs up to 10MB
  • Auto-tagged "Processed" once OCR finishes, "AI Processed" once insights are extracted
  • Searchable across every document by name, content, or date
  • Encrypted in transit and at rest, visible only to the caregivers you authorize

Care Log

A simple, tagged log of the small moments that matter.

Care Log is for the things that wouldn't fit in a document: conversations, observations, behaviors, medications given. One entry at a time, optionally tagged, automatically dated. Over weeks and months, the entries become evidence.

  • One-tap entry from any screen, with no required fields beyond what happened
  • Optional tags: Medical, School, Insurance, Admin, Behavior, Medication
  • Up to 10,000 characters per entry, searchable across the entire log
  • Voice-to-text capture (coming soon) for quick logging in the car or at appointments
  • Entries feed AI Insights, which surfaces patterns over time

2Understand

Quiet analysis surfaces what matters

AI Insights doesn't live in its own tab. It runs behind every module, reading documents and notes as they arrive, then surfacing the right information in the right place, without ever asking the caregiver to "use the AI."

AI Insights

The work that happens between upload and alert.

When CaseFile finishes processing a document, AI Insights reads it for structure: dates, deadlines, dollar amounts, authorization codes, medical events. Those structured details flow into Watchtower as alerts, into CaseMaker as sources, and into the search bar as findable facts.

  • Reads: PDFs, scanned images, forms, denial letters, EOBs, IEPs
  • Extracts: appeal deadlines, renewal dates, authorization codes, visit numbers, prescription changes
  • Surfaces patterns: across Care Log entries, including sleep changes, behavior shifts, and medication concerns
  • Flags inconsistencies: medication changes that weren't logged, conflicting authorizations
  • Stays inside your data: each insight is tied to a specific source document or note you can review

3Act

Reminders arrive on time. Drafts appear on request.

Watchtower watches in the background. CaseMaker waits to be asked. Together, they turn captured-and-understood information into the two things a caregiver actually needs: a heads-up before something matters, and a draft of the paperwork that has to go out.

Watchtower

Every deadline, before it's an emergency.

Watchtower pulls every appeal window, renewal date, authorization expiration, and appointment into one timeline. Items color themselves by urgency. Categories tell you which life-area the deadline belongs to. Nothing arrives on you by surprise.

  • Auto-extracted from documents in CaseFile by AI Insights
  • Grouped: Due Soon, Upcoming, Completed
  • Categorized: Medical, Appeal, Insurance, School, Benefits, Admin
  • Color-coded urgency: red (this week), amber (this month), green (later)
  • Manual entries supported for anything that didn't come from a document
The Medicare appeal for home health PT shows up in Watchtower with 5 days left, color-coded red because Sarah hasn't opened CaseMaker on it yet. One tap launches the draft with the right sources pre-selected.

CaseMaker

The paperwork, drafted from your file.

CaseMaker turns the documents in CaseFile and the entries in Care Log into the advocacy paperwork a caregiver would otherwise stay up late writing. Pick from common document types, upload a blank form to auto-fill, or describe a custom document. Each draft pulls only from the sources you select.

  • Insurance: Appeal Letter, Prior Authorization Request
  • Education: IEP Parent Input Letter, IEP Accommodation Request
  • Benefits: SSI/SSDI Application Narrative, Medicaid Application/Renewal
  • Medical: Provider Coordination Letter, Medical Records Request, Appointment Prep
  • Form fill: upload any blank form (Medicaid renewal, school enrollment, insurance claim) and CaseMaker auto-fills it from your CaseFile data
  • Custom drafts: describe what you need, and CaseMaker drafts a new document grounded in the sources you select

✦Built on your CaseFile

The stronger your CaseFile, the sharper everything else.

Hesvara's power comes from the records you keep. Every document you forward into CaseFile and every entry you make in Care Log strengthens what Watchtower can flag and what CaseMaker can draft. AI Insights is the connective layer reading what arrives and surfacing what matters.

01

CaseFile and Care Log capture the details

Safely store records in your healthcare-grade CaseFile, and capture observations, behaviors, and daily notes through Care Log. Search across the whole record to surface past incidents and histories when you need them.

02

CaseMaker and Watchtower turn history into supported next steps

Using the records in your CaseFile and Care Log, Watchtower tracks and flags critical dates and reminders. CaseMaker searches your documents and logs for relevant evidence to generate grounded appeals, letters, and applications.

03

AI Insights connects them all

AI Insights reads every new document and note as it arrives, extracting dates that flow into Watchtower and structured content that fuels CaseMaker. Without it, you'd have folders. With it, you have a working casefile.
